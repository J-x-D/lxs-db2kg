Mistral AI is a French company in artificial intelligence. It was founded in April 2023 by researchers previously employed by Meta and Google: Arthur Mensch, Timothée Lacroix and Guillaume Lample. It has raised 385 million euros, or about $415 million in October 2023. In December 2023, it attained a valuation of more than $2 billion. It produces open large language models, citing the foundational importance of open-source software, and as a response to proprietary models. As of December 2023, two models have been published, and are available as weights. Another prototype (Mistral Medium) is available via API only.
