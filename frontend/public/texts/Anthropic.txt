Anthropic PBC is an American artificial intelligence (AI) company founded in 2021 by former members of OpenAI. It specializes in the development of general AI systems and language models and is committed to the responsible use of AI. Anthropic is a public benefit corporation registered in the US state of Delaware. Products such as the chatbot Claude are designed to comply with generally recognized ethical rules through specific training in accordance with the principles of the United Nations, among others. Anthropic therefore describes its developments as Constitutional Artificial Intelligence, which is based on a constitution of human values. Early backers included Alameda Research and Google. Google has invested nearly $400 million in the company.
